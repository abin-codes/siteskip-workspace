Privacy Policy for SiteSkip

Effective Date: July 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SiteSkip. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SiteSkip Chrome Extension ("the Extension"). We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

2. Data We Collect

To provide AI-powered semantic navigation, SiteSkip temporarily collects the following information during active use:

  • Webpage Data (DOM): When you execute a search, the Extension scans the active webpage to extract available URLs and link text.
  • User Queries: The text query you type into the extension to specify your navigation intent.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is strictly used for core functionality. The extracted links and your query are sent securely to our external backend server via an encrypted connection (HTTPS). This data is processed by our AI engine to determine the most accurate destination URL on the current page. We do not store, log, or save your webpage data, queries, or browsing history on our servers after the navigation request is resolved.

4. Third-Party Services

SiteSkip utilizes third-party AI services to process natural language intents. Your queries and the extracted page links are securely transmitted to these external providers solely for the purpose of resolving navigation targets. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. User Consent

By installing and using SiteSkip, you consent to the temporary collection and processing of your active tab's link structure and search queries as described in this policy.
